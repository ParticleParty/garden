Here we go!

I saw a video last week of a developer who had been laid off from Atlassian. He spent 40 minutes quietly diagramming eight years of his life from memory. It struck me how ephemeral our careers are if we don't take the time to chronicle them ourselves. 

That video can be found here:
[I was laid off by Atlassian](https://youtu.be/55pTFVoclvE?si=OG_pQ8KQ8ApuwiPf)

We build complex systems every day, yet we rarely build systems to document our own growth. 

I set up this garden to fix that. I consume a vast amount of research trying to understand where this industry is heading. In a space that is currently defined by hype and reactivity, I have found that my most valuable trait is simply keeping a level head. 

This garden is where I will maintain that balance. It is a place to:
* Chronicle my technical work, the successes, and the failures.
* Distill complex AI research into grounded, actionable truths.
* Build a permanent record of my career that belongs entirely to me.

Everything starts here.
